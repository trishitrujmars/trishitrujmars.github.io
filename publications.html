Long-term and multi-stage ice accumulation in the martian mid-latitudes during the Amazonian

Trishit Ruj; Hanaya Okuda; Goro Komatsu; Hitoshi Hasegawa; James W. Head; Tomohiro Usui; Shun Mihira; Makito Kobayashi
Geology 2025 Mars Glaciation

This work reconstructs long-term and multi-stage ice accumulation in the Martian mid-latitudes and provides new insight into Amazonian climate variability and preservation of near-surface ice.
