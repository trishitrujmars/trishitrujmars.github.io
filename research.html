Research Areas

Past Research

Our previous work has focused on:

  • Mapping ancient Martian deltas to infer global ocean regression.
  • Crater retention age modeling for mid-latitude glaciation on Mars.
  • Lobate scarp tectonics and stress modeling on the Moon.
  • Spectral classification of hydrated minerals using Earth analog sites.
